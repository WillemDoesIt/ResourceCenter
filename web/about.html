About This Resource Center

Project Details

This project serves to connect people in Spokane to essential services.

The project was started by two brothers who wanted an easy, open way for immigrants, impoverished people, and anyone else to find and share local resources, we hope this will expand to help far more people. Itâ€™s mainly used by local organizations to guide people toward the help they need, quickly and privately.

Technical information

The website runs entirely on the user end, with no sign-in, ads, or tracking. Once opened, it keeps working even with slow or unstable internet even on the slowest of devices. Everything is open source (public domain) and free for anyone to reuse or expand upon.

You can learn more about the project in the githubs README and Documentation.


If you want to contribute and aren't a developer, please give feedback at ResourceCenterSpokane@gmail.com
